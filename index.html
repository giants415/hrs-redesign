House Rabbit Society: Bay Area

In 1988, Marinell Harriman founded the House Rabbit Society in Richmond, CA with a mission to prevent the exploitation of rabbits. Our mission is to help rabbits find loving, long-term homes through a fostering program and education about the benefits of housing rabbits indoors.

Over the past 40 years, HRS has grown from 300 members to over 8,000 with chapters in 37 states and seven other countries! More than 30,000 rabbits that were scheduled to be euthanized or deemed "unadoptable" have found new homes.
